Title:Unordinary Life

Medium: Maya

Dimensions: 1920x1080p

Title: Colorful Life Medium: Maya, Meshmixer Using free 3D models from Sketchfab, I created this 3D mashup scene in Maya and added color in Meshmixer. This scene is inspired by my late grandfather and the visual elements are inspired from a song associated with remembering loved ones who have passed. While the monochrome elements represent the unordinary and abstract, the colorful elements represent beauty and a short existence. The vibrancy of the flowers contrast the monochrome zebra and checkerboard, yet aesthetically compliment one another and create a balance suggesting both elements cannot exist without the other.
