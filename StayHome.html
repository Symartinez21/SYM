Title: Stay Home (Demo)

Medium: Premiere Pro, Photoshop, DSLR Camera, iPhoneXR

Dimensions: 1920 x 1080p

I wanted to apply the video editing knowledge I've learned throughout the Spring 2020 semester to create this video of what would be a demo for a video game. I tried this adventure game concept in my previous project and wanted to expand on it. The music comes from old classic video games that I grew up playing, and the graphics are inspired by video games for the Nintendo Gameboy. The on-screen graphics were created in Photoshop, and the video edited in Premiere. I alternated between using my Canon EOS DSLR and my iphone to record the video to broaden my range of quality. Using the DSLR I wanted more depth of field and different colors that I feel would be flat on iphone. Adding the video footage together in premiere was fun and I enjoyed filming and acting for this project!
