Title: Work As Usual (2021)

Medium: Clip Studio Paint

Dimensions: 1920x1080p

This digitally drawn 2D looping animation was inspired by Youtube videos that livestream chill lo-fi music with relaxing and complimentary animations. In comparison to animating in Photoshop, I had the creative freedom to add more details and "paint" through Clip Studio Paint. This piece also reflects the work-from-home status due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I plan to continue polishing this animation and gradually add more minor movements; for instance letting the clouds roll by in the background and show the dog in the corner silently breathing as he sleeps.
