About Me

My name is Sophia, I am born and raised in the South Bay area of California. Animation and illustration has been my passion since I was in elementary school. My artwork is heavily inspired by the pop culture I absorbed from my childhood growing up watching anime and Cartoon Network. I also find aesthetic inspiration from the low-poly nostalgic look of games from the Nintendo 64!
