Transience: Capturing the Subtle Things in Life

Year: 2020 Medium: Full HD Color Video, 00"02"14 Description: My work was inspired by the style of video captured in vlogs on youtube and stories on social media found on Instagram and Snapchat. My goal was to create an aesthetically pleasing video and experimenting with color correction. What I have learned to appreciate about vlogs is that the footage captured means something to the videographer, and that they wish to share their vision and experience with others.
