Title: Kanye Quest Trailer (WIP)

Medium: TvPaint, Adobe Animate, Adobe Premiere, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects

Dimensions: 1920x1080p

While I attended DeAnza College, I took three semesters to plan, draft and produce this short animation. The inspiration for this project literally came to me in a dream with a random yet strong plot that I strongly desired to visualize through animation. This “trailer” for an action-packed movie is 2D animated switching between using Adobe Animate and TvPaint, digitally drawn in Adobe Photoshop, utilized Adobe After Effects and pieced together using Adobe Premiere. I struggled finding animation software that ran smoothly on my Mac and could keep up with my PC, so I felt that the production of my project would have been more complete if I had the consistent working tools. I wish more animation programs could run on mac, and were affordable! If my resources were consistent and reliable then this would be polished.
