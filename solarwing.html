Title: Solarwing, 2022

solarwing by scribbledstar on Sketchfab

Medium: Blender

A spaceship I created inspired by the arwing from Star Fox 64. I designed the ship from memory as I was building it. This was an assignment for Art 107 as a more dynamic approach than the starter donut model.
This is the first 3D model I have ever made in Blender and it was a lot of fun to animate as well. The ball in the back is reminiscent of the energy shots when building up in the game.
For the assignment prompt, I had to give the ship a purpose in a solarpunk future. Being aware of the amount of waste humanity creates, I feel like the ship can fly around and collect garbage that gravitates towards the sphere.
